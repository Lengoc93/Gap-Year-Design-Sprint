# Maintenance Protocol — How to Keep the Manual Alive

> **Purpose:** Without maintenance, this manual becomes a snapshot of who you were, not who you are. This protocol ensures it stays current.
> **When:** Monthly + after big events

---

## Monthly Review (1-2 hours, first Sunday of the month)

### Step 1: Read the compass (5 min)

Read the compass sentence: **Sovereignty (root) → Diversification (strategy) → Excellence (standard)**

- Does this still feel true?
- Has the order shifted?
- Has a new value emerged that isn't captured?

If yes to any → update `who-is-ngoc.md`

### Step 2: Scan the personality radar (10 min)

Go through each dimension in `who-is-ngoc.md`:

| Check | Question |
|---|---|
| Layer 1 scores | Has any core drive score changed? (Evidence needed, not just feeling.) |
| Layer 2 expressions | Am I expressing my personality differently than last month? |
| Layer 3 trauma dimensions | Has any trauma dimension moved on the spectrum? (red → yellow → green) |
| Professional Ngoc | Has my professional identity shifted? New skills, new direction, new positioning? |
| Non-professional Ngoc | Has my social architecture changed? New people? Lost people? Relationship status? |

### Step 4: Check the anti-patterns (10 min)

Go through the anti-patterns in `what-ngoc-needs.md`:

| Check | Question |
|---|---|

| Was I able to catch them in real-time? | If yes → pattern is weakening. If no → needs structural intervention. |

| Any anti-pattern that no longer activates? | Move it to a "resolved" section. Don't delete — it's history. |

### Step 5: Check the life phase (5 min)

Read your current blueprint in `blueprints-by-life-phase.md`:

| Check | Question |
|---|---|
| Am I still in the same phase? | [placeholder] |

### Step 6: Check the decision protocol (5 min)

Skim the decision flow path in `decision-flow-path.md`:

| Check | Question |
|---|---|
| Did I use the protocol this month? | If not, why? Too complex? No decisions? Or avoided it? |
| Did any decision bypass the protocol? | Was that the right call or a shortcut? |
| Do the 9 compass questions still feel sharp? | If any feel stale or irrelevant, replace them. |

### Step 7: Write the update (15 min)

For each section that changed, update the file directly. Include:
- What changed
- Why (evidence from the month)
- Date of update

## Big Event Review (30-60 min, within 48 hours of the event)

### What counts as a "big event"

- [placeholder: Ngoc to define — examples might include:]
- A major decision was made (career move, relationship change, relocation)
- A therapeutic breakthrough (new trauma layer processed, new pattern identified)
- A somatic collapse or significant activation (validation wound, panic, freeze)
- A life phase transition (started a job, ended a relationship, became a caregiver)
- A financial milestone or shock (runway change, major expense, income change)
- [placeholder: anything else Ngoc considers "big enough" to warrant a manual check]

### Big Event Protocol

1. **Name the event** — one sentence.

2. **Which part of the manual does it affect?**
   - [ ] Who is Ngoc (identity shifted?)
   - [ ] What Ngoc needs (new need discovered? Old need changed?)
   - [ ] Blueprints (phase changed? Phase blueprint proved wrong?)
   - [ ] Decision protocol (protocol used? Protocol failed? Protocol needs updating?)

3. **What changed?** — Write it down. Be specific.

4. **Update the relevant file(s)** — Don't just note the change. Actually edit the document.

5. **Date it** — Every update gets a date so you can track evolution over time.

## Signals That the Manual Needs an Unscheduled Update

Don't wait for the monthly review if:

- [ ] You catch yourself making a decision that contradicts the compass and you feel conflicted about it
- [ ] A somatic red flag fires and you don't find it in the anti-patterns list
- [ ] Someone new becomes important in your life (or someone important leaves)
- [ ] You realize you've been ignoring a non-negotiable rhythm for 2+ weeks
- [ ] A therapeutic session produces a breakthrough that reframes a Layer 3 dimension
- [ ] You read the manual and think "this doesn't sound like me anymore"
- [ ] [placeholder: other signals Ngoc identifies over time]

---

## What NOT to Update

- Don't update scores without evidence. "I feel like my sovereignty is lower" is not enough. "I've accepted 3 external impositions this month without pushback" is evidence.
- Don't remove anti-patterns that still activate. Move them to "resolved" only when they haven't fired for 3+ months.
- Don't rewrite history. If a past test run or calibration produced insight, keep it. It's part of the record.
- Don't update during a somatic collapse or emotional storm. Wait 24-48 hours. The manual is for the regulated self, not the activated self.
