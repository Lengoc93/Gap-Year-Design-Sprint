## Research Notes

### The company — Masan Group

**Business structure (2025):**
- **WinCommerce (WCM):** Retail arm. 4,597 stores (WinMart, WinMart+). 2025 revenue: VND 38,979B (+18.3% YoY). Profit VND 501B (87× YoY). 90.3% of stores above store-level EBITDA breakeven. Net 764 new stores in 2025.
- **Masan Consumer Holdings (MCH):** Branded FMCG. 2024 revenue VND 30.9T (+9.4%). Retail Supreme distribution rollout: 420,000 POS (+70%). Post-distribution restructuring recovery in progress.
- **Masan MEATLife (MML):** Meat processing. 2025 revenue VND 9,230B (+20.7%). NPAT 619B (~25× YoY).
- **Masan High-Tech Materials (MHT):** Critical minerals / tungsten. "Back to Basics" strategy (cost discipline, operational excellence).
- **Phúc Long:** Coffee / tea chain (acquired). Under Masan brand umbrella.
- **Techcombank (associated):** Major financial partner — "Banking at Your Fingertips" deployed at Win stores.

**Financial performance:**
- FY2024 net revenue: VND 83.2T (~$3.28B), +6.3% YoY
- FY2024 NPAT pre-MI: VND 4.3T. 2024 Q4 profit: nearly 14× YoY.
- 2025 guidance (updated): Revenue VND 80–85.5T, NPAT VND 4.8–6.5T (+14–52%)
- 1H2025: ~2× YoY earnings, surpassed interim guidance

**Strategic vision — "Point of Life":**
- Serve 50–100M Vietnamese consumers as the single destination for essential daily needs (grocery, financial, lifestyle/health)
- O2O integration: offline retail (Win stores) + online data platform (WIN Membership + app)
- Tech bets: Digital Twin (value chain simulation), WIN AI Smart POS, AI + Big Data for supply chain and personalization
- Consumer fintech: Techcombank partnership (banking at Win stores), Trusting Social partnership (AI-powered credit, demand forecasting)
- WINLife stores: 27 multi-utility flagship stores combining grocery + pharmacy + financial services + F&B

**Trusting Social strategic partnership (2022, $65M Series C lead):**
- Masan invested $65M for 25% of Trusting Social via Sherpa Company Ltd
- Mission: develop AI-powered consumer tech platform serving 27M Vietnamese families
- Product scope: personalized fintech products (credit, BNPL, financial services) + AI for retail store selection, demand/supply planning, product assortment

- **Critical insight for Ngoc:** She built products at TS that directly power what Masan is building. She is not pitching capability — she is proving it with the actual partner company's work.

**Technology & data strategy:**
- WIN Membership platform: unified consumer identity across WinMart/WinMart+ chains

- Digital Twin: simulation model for full value chain optimization (brand → retail → consumer)
- Retail Supreme (MCH): end-to-end digital supply chain digitalization — general trade, supply & demand planning, salesman productivity, marketing ROI
