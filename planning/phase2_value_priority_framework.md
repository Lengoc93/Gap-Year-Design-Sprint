### G5 Egg Freezing ROI Framework

```
INPUTS:
- Current age: 33
- AMH level: [from W4 consultation — update if retested at G8]
- Cost per cycle: [SIH pricing from W4 research]
- Expected viable eggs per cycle at 33 vs. 34 vs. 35
- Storage cost/year
- Natural conception probability: 33 (~75%/year) → 35 (~66%) → 37 (~55%) → 40 (~44%)

SCENARIOS:
A) Freeze 1 cycle at 33 (2026) — partner within 3 years → natural conception + frozen backup
B) Freeze 1 cycle at 33 (2026) — no partner by 36 → use frozen eggs
C) Delay to 34 (2027) — same scenarios with reduced egg quality (~15% decline)
D) Skip freezing — accept natural probability curve

DECISION VARIABLES:
- Financial cost vs. optionality value
- Current relationship trajectory (Swaraj transparency: no marriage in 1 year)
- Location dependency (G2: which clinic? local vs. international?)

OUTPUT: "Freeze in [month] at [clinic]" OR "Defer to [trigger date/event]"
```
