### Format Discovery: Position Paper (Wed 25 — Block #3)

> **Context:** Full thesis format feels overwhelming for the trust/compliance research topic. Academic rigor is wanted, but the institutional PhD container may not be the right fit (see nordic scorecard 1.5 signal). A lighter-weight academic format is needed to "skin in the game" without committing to a 3-4 year container.
>
> **Position paper** (~2,000-3,000 words): Academic structure (abstract, argument, evidence, implications) without requiring full methodology, data collection, or exhaustive literature review. Stakes a claim based on existing evidence and reasoning. Used by academics to test ideas before committing to a full study.
>
> **Publishing options (beginner-friendly, public-facing):**
> - **SSRN preprint** — academic credibility without peer review gatekeeping. Can post a working paper, get citations/feedback. Free.
> - **The Conversation** style — academic rigor written for non-academic readers (~800-1,500 words). Requires institutional affiliation (barrier).
> - **Medium / Substack long-form** — no gatekeeping, public, discoverable. Can build audience. Not "academic" but respected for thought leadership.
> - **Blog post on personal site** — full control, can link from LinkedIn/CV.
>
> **Recommended path:** Start with position paper format. Publish on SSRN (for academic cred) + Medium/Substack (for reach). If the writing energy sustains across 2-3 position papers → that's the depth endurance signal for research viability WITHOUT requiring the PhD institution.
