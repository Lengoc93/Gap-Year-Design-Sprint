> **Upside:** Real-world context for evaluating the business partnership transition. Free local guidance removes navigation friction. Partial food/transport coverage reduces daily cash burn. Authentic cultural immersion no guidebook replicates.
>
> **Risk:** Traveling to Raj's home state while the romantic → business transition is unsettled introduces emotional ambiguity. A trip that ends with unresolved relationship tension on June 12 → HN arrive June 15 → Vin Day 1 soon after = compounded emotional load entering the highest-stakes career moment of the year.
>
> **Decision test:** Is this India trip primarily about growth / culture / business exploration, or is it about Raj? If the answer is "both," be explicit about which one leads. If it's "Raj," then India's value ratio drops further because the emotional cost isn't priced in.
