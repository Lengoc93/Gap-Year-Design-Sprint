## 4. Income Analysis — PIT + SI Calculations

### 4.1 Vietnam PIT & Social Insurance — Calculation Framework

**Vietnam PIT 2026 brackets** (employment income, after deductions) — **5-bracket system effective 2026** (per Biểu thuế TNCN lũy tiến 2026 — Bậc thuế 5):

| Bậc | Taxable Income Band (monthly) | Tax Rate | Max Tax on Band |
|:---:|---|:---:|---:|
| 1 | 0 – 10,000,000 | 5% | 500,000 |
| 2 | 10,000,001 – 30,000,000 | 10% | 2,000,000 |
| 3 | 30,000,001 – 60,000,000 | 20% | 6,000,000 |
| 4 | 60,000,001 – 100,000,000 | 30% | 12,000,000 |
| 5 | 100,000,001+ | 35% | 35% of amount over 100M |

> ✅ **Cross-validated against TS payslip (Feb 2026):** PIT = 6,716,957 on taxable base of ~51.1M (Bậc 1: 500K + Bậc 2: 2,000K + Bậc 3: 21.1M × 20% = 4,216,957 = total 6,716,957 ✓). The 5-bracket system was already in effect for Feb 2026 payroll. **Note:** The old 7-bracket system (with 15% and 25% bands) no longer applies — all Vin gross→net calculations use the 5-bracket table.

**Personal deductions — updated per Luật TNCN số 109/2025/QH15 (effective 01/01/2026):**
- Personal allowance: **15,500,000 VND/month** (was 11M under old law)
- Dependent deduction (1 dependent — mẹ): **6,200,000 VND/month** (was 4.4M)
- **Total pre-PIT deduction (1 dependent): 21,700,000 VND/month**

**All-insurance (BHXH + BHYT + BHTN) employee contribution at Vingroup:**
- Rate: 10.5% total (BHXH 8% + BHYT 1.5% + BHTN 1%)
- **Vingroup declares ALL insurances (BHXH + BHYT + BHTN) on 70% of gross salary** — not 100%. So at 140M gross, the declared insurance base = 70% × 140M = 98M.
- **BHXH (8%) + BHYT (1.5%) cap**: 20 × lương cơ sở = 20 × 2,340,000 = **46,800,000 VND/month** — both are capped here for any gross ≥ 67M
- **BHTN (1%) cap**: 20 × lương tối thiểu vùng I = 20 × 4,960,000 = **99,200,000 VND/month** — much higher cap, so BHTN = 1% × (70% × gross) for all T4+ salaries (since 70% × 140M = 98M < 99.2M)
- **Employee insurance formula (Vùng I, 70% Vin base, gross ≥ 67M):**
  - BHXH: 8% × 46,800,000 = 3,744,000 (capped)
  - BHYT: 1.5% × 46,800,000 = 702,000 (capped)
  - BHTN: 1% × (70% × gross) — varies with gross
  - **Total = 4,446,000 + (0.7% × gross)**
- At T5 (60M gross): 70% × 60M = 42M < BHXH/BHYT cap → all three on 42M: 10.5% × 42M = **4,410,000**

> ⚠️ **TS Payroll Exception (payslip confirmed):** TrustingSocial's payroll structure is non-standard. The Feb 2026 payslip shows: BHXH (8%) = **0**, BHYT (1.5%) = **702,000**, BHTN (1%) = **0** — total employee SI = **702,000 only** (not the standard 4,914,000). TS effectively absorbed the employee's BHXH and BHTN share (~4,212,000/month). Additionally, non-taxable allowances in the gross structure significantly reduce the PIT base, resulting in PIT of only 6,716,957 on 76.7M+ gross income. **This TS-specific arrangement will NOT apply at Vingroup.** At Vin, full 10.5% SI (4,914,000/month at cap) will be deducted.
